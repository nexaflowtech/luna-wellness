Quinoa & Avocado Power Bowl

A nutrient-dense bowl designed to support estrogen metabolism and sustained energy levels.

485 Kcal
18g Protein
52g Carbs
24g Fats

Ingredients

2 Servings
psychiatry

Organic Quinoa

1 cup, cooked

eco

Ripe Avocado

1 medium, sliced

nutrition

Baby Spinach

2 cups, fresh

egg

Pumpkin Seeds

2 tablespoons, toasted

Preparation

01

Base Prep

Rinse the quinoa thoroughly and cook according to package instructions. Let it cool slightly and fluff with a fork.

02

Veggie Assembly

Massage the baby spinach with a drop of lemon juice and olive oil. Slice the avocado into thin wedges.

03

Final Touch

Combine quinoa, spinach, and avocado. Top with pumpkin seeds and a light lemon-tahini dressing.
